Comprehension Questions

There are two phases to this game: nighttime and daytime.

During nighttime, only the mafia can speak and act.

Participants will be eliminated during each nighttime and daytime by voting.

The mafia win if they are equal in number to the bystanders. The bystanders win if they eliminate the mafia.
