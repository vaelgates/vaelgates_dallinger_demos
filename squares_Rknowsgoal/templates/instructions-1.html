Instructions

This game has 12 rounds. In each round, you will see a goal pattern you are trying to complete, and the main 3x3 grid. Your task is to take turns choosing squares in the main grid with a partner until you complete the goal pattern. Your partner will change each round.

Both you and your partner can see the goal pattern. Your partner will try to help you. You will always go first.

There is no time limit to complete the pattern: you choose a square, then wait for your partner to choose a square, then you can choose a square again, etc. Your partner will be different for each round. You will receive a bonus that decreases based on the number of mistakes you and your various partners make.
